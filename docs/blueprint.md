# **App Name**: LawAI

## Core Features:

- Document Summarization: Summarize legal documents. Users can upload or paste text, and the AI will provide a concise summary, saving time and improving understanding. The LLM tool uses reasoning to decide when or if to incorporate some piece of information in its output.
- Legal Term Definition: Generate definitions of legal terms. Users can input specific legal terms, and the AI will provide clear, understandable definitions sourced from reliable legal databases. The LLM tool uses reasoning to decide when or if to incorporate some piece of information in its output.
- Drag & Drop Upload: Easy document upload via drag and drop.
- Clear Result Presentation: Clean, readable display of results, highlighting key information.
- Client-Side Processing: Secure, client-side processing of documents for enhanced privacy. No data is persisted, except temporarily in the browser.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep indigo (#4B0082) to convey trust and authority.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F0F0) to ensure readability and a professional feel.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) for interactive elements and call to action buttons, offering a sense of calm and precision.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for readability and a modern feel.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (serif) to provide an elegant, intellectual feel to headlines.
- Use crisp, professional icons for navigation and document actions.
- Ensure a clean, intuitive layout with a clear visual hierarchy for easy navigation.
